Introducing Collision Hours

The best things happen when people are running into each other and sharing ideas. Meet other Chicago founders, share ideas, and have fun at Collision Hours.

Rules of Collision Hours

  1. Do not talk about Collision Hours. Invite other awesome founders with cool ideas.

  2. If this is your first Collision Hour, you have to pitch your startup in 30 seconds.

  3. Don't try to "network" with people. Just have fun. The dots will connect themselves later.
